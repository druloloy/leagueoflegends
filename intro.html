What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a team strategy game created by Riot Games where you work together in a team of 5 against 5 other opposing players to destroy each other’s “Nexus” to win the match.

Our Purpose

This research will be focused more on finding evidence to prove if visual effects are an effective way of attracting players to buy skins. The result will be favorable both to Riot Games and all other players who support the game.

About Us
